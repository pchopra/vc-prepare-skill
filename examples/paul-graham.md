## Your Game Plan

### How They Think

Paul Graham evaluates in the first 60 seconds. He fires rapid questions to understand what you actually do, not what you think you do. He values conciseness over polish: if you can't explain VC Whisper in one sentence, he'll assume you don't understand your own product. He gets excited about tools that remove friction from processes everyone accepts as painful, and he probes hard on "why now" and "why you." He famously hates slides. Just have a conversation.

### Where You Connect

- **You built a tool for founders**
  PG has written repeatedly that founder tools are undervalued. VC Whisper is a tool built by founders who experienced the pain of unprepared investor meetings. This is the exact origin story PG looks for.

- **62K organic launch**
  PG's primary signal at pre-seed is organic demand. 62K people watched the launch tweet. 174 likes. 20 retweets. No paid marketing. This is "making something people want" in its purest form.

- **The stack is the moat**
  VC Whisper combines Exa, Seedlist, xAI, and Browserbase into a single workflow that would take a founder hours to replicate manually. The integration layer is the product.

- **Expand beyond prep**
  VC Whisper started with pitch analysis, then added Prepare. The trajectory is clear: full fundraising lifecycle. PG loves wedge strategies that start narrow and expand.

- **AI-native, not AI-wrapped**
  VC Whisper orchestrates multiple AI services with domain-specific prompt engineering. PG distinguishes between "uses AI" and "is AI."

### They'll Probably Ask

- **"What do you actually do?"**
  One sentence: "Founders enter an investor's name, and we generate a complete briefing: their career, portfolio, relevant tweets, and a game plan for the meeting." Then shut up and let him ask follow-ups.

- **"Why would someone pay for this when they can just Google the investor?"**
  Because Googling takes 2 hours and misses the connections. VC Whisper cross-references their portfolio, recent tweets, and your specific company in 60 seconds. Show the Paul Graham example output. The depth speaks for itself.

- **"What's the bigger play here?"**
  Fundraising is broken. Founders waste months meeting wrong-fit investors. We started with meeting prep because it's the sharpest pain point. Next: investor discovery, then CRM, then the full fundraising stack. Every founder raises money; the TAM is every startup.

- **"How do you make money?"**
  Freemium. Free tier with basic briefings. Pro tier ($49/mo) with API-enriched data, PDF exports, unlimited briefings. Enterprise tier for accelerators and law firms.

### Don't Say

- **Avoid: Don't lead with "AI-powered"**
  PG has been publicly skeptical of startups that lead with "AI" as a differentiator. Lead with the problem and the result. Let him discover the AI is sophisticated.

- **Avoid: Don't compare to Crunchbase or LinkedIn Sales Navigator**
  PG thinks in terms of novel categories, not competitive positioning. VC Whisper isn't a better database; it's a new workflow.
